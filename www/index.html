About Us

Our story

Nothing but Nothing was founded with one belief - The best things in life are free. So we set out on a journey to find exactly what people want. Surprisingly we found people like to spend money, but sadly they never actually have anything to spend their money on. So we ditched out previous belief and changed it to - The best things in life are the ones you don't own. Voila, we had a company!

The next question was what as a company would we sell? It was at that moment that someone in the board meeting finished their cup of coffee. "I guess the glass is all empty," someone from the opposite side of the table chortled. Urika! We had our product. It was so simple, we could sell people the lack of something! Excitedly, we all picked up our pens and began jotting down ideas for future products; Products like, the bottle of nothing, or our personal favorite, box of nothing.

Ten minutes after that faithful day and our company has been a long time success. We went from a company that only sold two items of nothing, to a company with a repertoire of 5 completely original and unique products. It is our humble belief that for many years people will be buying the one thing they do not presently have: Nothing!

Our Commitment

As a company, we realize that our money comes from one place, the pockets connected to the pants of the individuals who decide to buy our product. As such our primary focus is to get as much of that money from you as possible. You can count on us because we have a proven system that works. The PROMISE system.

  • Products to sell for money
  • Results to measure our money
  • Orders to get our money
  • Money to add to our money
  • Involvement in taking your money
  • Separation of you from your money
  • Excellence at taking your money

As you can see, our primary focus is on you, you and your delicious money. As long as you have money, we are more than willing to take it from you. That is a promise you can trust, that is the PROMISE system.
